Privacy Policy — LOSAI

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This policy describes how LOSAI ("the Service") handles information. The Service is a personal tool used by its author to publish content to their own social channels.

1. Data processed

To publish videos to the author's TikTok account, the Service obtains via OAuth an access token and basic profile information (the open_id identifier, display name, avatar) provided by the TikTok APIs. This data is used only to identify the connected account and to upload videos.

2. Where it is stored

The access token and basic data are stored locally, on a private device owned by the author, in protected form. They are not transmitted to any third-party servers, nor to any service other than TikTok itself.

3. Use of data

Data is used solely to: (a) authenticate the author's TikTok account; (b) upload videos as a draft or post to that same account. It is not used for profiling, advertising, or any other purpose.

4. Sharing

The Service does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. The only data exchange is with the official TikTok APIs, as required for it to function.

5. Retention and revocation

The token is kept only as long as needed for publishing. The author can revoke the app's access at any time from their TikTok account settings, or by deleting the locally stored token.

6. Third-party data

The Service does not collect or process personal data of third-party users or of the channels' followers.

7. Contact

For privacy-related requests: losaianimali@gmail.com
